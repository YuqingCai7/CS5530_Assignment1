VARIABLE DESCRIPTIONS

-------------------------------------------------------------------

Variable:                                 The variable indicates:

-------------------------------------------------------------------


Height                                    The height of the participant measured in inches


Weight                                    The weight of the participant measured in pounds


Age                                       The age of the participant measured in years


Grip strength                             The grip strength of the participant measured in kilograms


Frailty                                   The qualitative attribute indicated the presence(Y) or absence(N) of the symptoms
