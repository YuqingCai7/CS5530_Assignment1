VARIABLE DESCRIPTIONS

-------------------------------------------------------------------

Variable:                                 The variable indicates:

-------------------------------------------------------------------

gender                                    Indicates whether the participant is a male or a female


race/ethnicity                            The particular race/ethnic group the participant identified with


parental level of education               The highest level of education completed by the participant's parents


lunch                                     Whether the participant was eligible for standard or free/reduced lunch


test preparation course                   Whether the participant has completed/not completed test preparation course 


math score                                The math score obtained by the participant


reading score                             The reading score obtained by the participant


writing score                             The writing score obtained by the participant
